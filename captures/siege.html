SIEGE

A game of territory for two players, one screen.


Setup: An 8×8 board. Red starts in two corners. Blue starts in the other two.


On your turn: Click one of your stones, then click an empty cell to move.

  • Close move (1 step in any direction): your stone clones — it stays and a copy appears.
  • Far move (2 steps, jumping): your stone leaps — it moves, leaving the old cell empty.

Conversion: After you move, all enemy stones adjacent to your landing cell switch to your color.


Win: When the board is full or neither player can move, the player with the most stones wins.


Pass: If you can't move, your turn is skipped.
